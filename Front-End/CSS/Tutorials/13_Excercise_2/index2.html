Phewa Lake, Pokhara, Nepal

Nature Lake

Discover the Tranquil Beauty of Phewa Lake

Phewa Lake, also known as Fewa Lake, is a stunning freshwater lake located in Pokhara, Nepal. It is the second-largest lake in Nepal and a popular tourist destination. Surrounded by the Annapurna and Machapuchare mountains, the lake offers breathtaking views and opportunities for boating and relaxation. The lake also features the Tal Barahi Temple, situated on an island in the middle, which adds to its cultural and religious significance. Boating on Phewa Lake and enjoying the picturesque scenery is a favorite activity for visitors to Pokhara.
